Understanding RTI

What is RTI?

The Right to Information Act empowers citizens to request information from public authorities, promoting transparency and accountability in governance.

Who Can File?

Any citizen of India can file an RTI application to obtain information from government departments, institutions, and organizations receiving government funds.
